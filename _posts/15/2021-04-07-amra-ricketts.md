## Who is Amra Ricketts
                  
                  
                  
Also well known as FLitz or The Nerdsworth, he is an artist, YouTube personality, singer, actor and dancer affiliated with Smosh Games. He is the founder of The Nerdiest Kids.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
