## Who is Kris Nava



Vocalist and guitarist for the Regional Mexican genre band Tercer Elemento, AKA T3R Elemento. Their song &#8220;Rafa Caro&#8221; was featured on Billboard&#8217;s Regional Mexican Songs Chart.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



His band would sign with the record label Parral Music.

                
                
                
## Random data



Bandmates of his would include Felipe Prieto on the requinto, Sergio Cardenas on the electric bass and tuba, and Zeus Gamez on the accordion.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kris Nava



His band was based out of Las Vegas and California. He has a brother named Gabriel Nava who&#8217;s also a musician. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Kris Nava



He and Rogelio Martinez are both notable as vocalists of Regional Mexican music.
