## Who is Keri Seavey



Most well recognized as the mother of American Idol competitor and Why Don&#8217;t We member Daniel Seavey. She became extremely popular on social media, earning over 200,000 followers on her Instagram account. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She graduated from Orange Coast College and would later go on to work at Living Water Church. 

                
                
                
## Random data



In May 2020, she did a sponsored Instagram post for the protein bar Built Bar. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Keri Seavey



She and Jeff Seavey had four children together: Daniel, Christian, Tyler, and Anna. She and Jeff ended their marriage in 2020, after 27 years together.

                
                
                
## People Related With Keri Seavey
