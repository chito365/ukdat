## Who is Kathy Bates



Actress whose films include About Schmidt, The Blind Side, Titanic and The Late Shift. She received a massive fan base for her performance as a disturbed nurse in the film version of the Stephen King&#8217;s novel Misery. She also plays Ethel Darling in FX&#8217;s American Horror Story. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She joined a sorority at Southern Methodist University. She debuted in the 1971 film Taking Off. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She has been nominated for nine Emmy Awards for her TV work and made a cameo appearance in The Stand.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kathy Bates



She was married to Tony Campisi from 1991 to 1997.

                
                
                
## People Related With Kathy Bates



She starred alongside James Caan in Misery.
