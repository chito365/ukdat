## Who is Kevin Franke



Assistant professor of civil engineering at Brigham Young University who is also the patriarch of the Franke family featured on the popular YouTube channel 8 Passengers. The family vlog channel has earned more than 2.4 million subscribers. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He graduated Magna Cum Laude from Utah State University with a Bachelor of Science degree in civil engineering. He then got his master&#8217;s in the field from the University of Washington before earning his PhD from Brigham Young University.

                
                
                
## Random data



He previously worked as a professional geotechnical/earthquake engineering consultant for close to 7 years.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kevin Franke



He and his wife Ruby are the parents of children named Shari, Chad, Abby, Julie, Russell and Eve.

                
                
                
## People Related With Kevin Franke
