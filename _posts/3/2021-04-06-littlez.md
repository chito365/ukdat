## Who is Littlez
                  
                  
                  
One-sixth of the English rap group Section Boyz that released the mixtape Don&#8217;t Panic in September of 2015. The mixtape peaked at #36 on the UK Albums Chart and helped them win the MOBO Award for Best Newcomer.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
                  
                  
                  
He and the rest of the Section Boyz came together thanks to UK rapper Ghost who created the group. Their debut mixtape was titled Sectionly.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Random data
                  
                  
                  
He performs alongside rappers who by the names of Swift, Deepee, Sleeks, Knine and Inch in Section Boyz.
