## Who is Ezra Koenig
                  
                  
                  
Lead vocalist, guitarist, and main songwriter for the the New York-based band Vampire Weekend, a band that became known for the hits &#8220;A-Punk,&#8221; &#8220;Cousins,&#8221; and &#8220;Diane Young.&#8221;
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity

## Random data
