## Who is Hughie Thomasson
                  
                  
                  
Guitarist for the southern rock band Lynyrd Skynyrd who started out as a songwriter and performer in the Outlaws.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
                  
                  
                  
He joined the Outlaws when it was still a struggling local band.
