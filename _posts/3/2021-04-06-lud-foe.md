## Who is Lud Foe
                  
                  
                  
Chicago drill rapper who had his biggest year in 2016 with the popular singles &#8220;Im Da Man&#8221; and &#8220;Cuttin Up,&#8221; the former of which was remixed by Lil Durk and G Herbo.
