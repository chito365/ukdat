## Who is Giacomo Gianniotti

## Prior to Popularity
                  
                  
                  
He grew up in Toronto, Canada and his first onscreen appearance was as the character Alberto Talenti on the series Medicina Generale. 
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Random data
