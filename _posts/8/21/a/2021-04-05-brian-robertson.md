## Who is  Brian Robertson
                  
                  
                  
Scottish guitarist who played with Thin Lizzy and Motorhead.

He grew up with formal musical training on the cello and piano, then was inspired by the rock &#8216;n&#8217; roll scene.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data
