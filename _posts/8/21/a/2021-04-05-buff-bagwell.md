## Who is  Buff Bagwell
                  
                  
                  
Known for his finishing move, the Buff Blockbuster, he was a 5-time WCW World Tag Team Champion.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He was born Marcus Alexander Bagwell and was an outstanding baseball player at Sprayberry High School.
