## Who is Zaya Sosho
                  
                  
                  
Also known as SuperSaiyan, he is a member of the dance crew The Future Kingz, who has appeared on World of Dance and The Ellen Show. He has been the choreographer for Ayleo and Mateo Bowles AKA Ayo & Teo.
