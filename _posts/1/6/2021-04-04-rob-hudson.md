## Who is  Rob Hudson
                  
                  
                  
Known for his time in Australia&#8217;s Victorian Legislative Assembly as a member of the Labor Party. He held the position between 2002 and 2010, and served in varying capacities during his tenure.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
