## Who is  Yangyang

## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He took home first place at an international KOD street dance competition in 2016.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data 
                  
                  
                  
He learned to speak German, English, Chinese and Korean fluently.
