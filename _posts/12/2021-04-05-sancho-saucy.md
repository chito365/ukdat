## Who is  Sancho Saucy
                  
                  
                  
One of two hip hop artists to comprise Sauce Twinz, working alongside his partner Sauce Walka. They would release mixtapes titled Sorry 4 the Sauce, Sorry 4 the Sauce 2 and In Sauce We Trust.
