## Who is Amy Hill
                  
                  
                  
American actress who played Mrs. DePaulo in That&#8217;s So Raven and appeared in the films 50 First Dates and The Cat in the Hat. In 2016 she portrayed Mama P in the Amazon series Just Add Magic.
