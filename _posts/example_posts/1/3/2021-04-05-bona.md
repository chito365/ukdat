## Who is  Bona
                  
                  
                  
Korean pop singer and member of 13-member K-pop girl group Cosmic Girls (aka WJSN), which was formed by Starship Entertainment and Yuehua Entertainment and originally separated into four subgroups.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
