## Who is  Natasha Slayton
                  
                  
                  
Known best as a member of the all-female group G.R.L., this pop vocalist and actress contributed to such hit singles as &#8220;Ugly Heart&#8221; and Pitbull&#8217;s &#8220;Wild Wild Love.&#8221; 
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
She joined Twitter in late 2009, shortly before G.R.L. rose to fame. By 2014, she had attracted more than 20,000 Twitter followers. 
                  
                
                
                
## Random data 
                  
                  
                  
Tragically, her G.R.L. bandmate Simone Battle committed suicide in 2014.
                  
                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Natasha Slayton
                  
                  
                  
Her father, Bobby Slayton, had a career as a comedian, actor, and screenwriter.
                  
                
                
                
## People Related With  Natasha Slayton
