## Who is  Ivan L. Moody
                  
                  
                  
Frontman for the metal band Five Finger Death Punch who is known by his pseudonym Ghost. He appeared in The Devil&#8217;s Carnival as well.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He joined his first major band, Motograter, in 2002.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data
