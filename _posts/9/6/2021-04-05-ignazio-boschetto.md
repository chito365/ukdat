## Who is  Ignazio Boschetto
                  
                  
                  
Tenor in the Italian operatic pop trio Il Volo, the group responsible for albums such as We Are Love, Mas Que Amor, and their self-titled debut album.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
