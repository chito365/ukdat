## Who is  Guy Fletcher
                  
                  
                  
Talented multi-instrumentalist who served as the keyboardist for the popular English rock band Dire Straights from 1984 to 1995. His debut solo album, Inamorata, was released in 2008.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
