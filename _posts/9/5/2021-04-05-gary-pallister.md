## Who is  Gary Pallister
                  
                  
                  
Center back who played for Manchester United from 1989-1998 and won four Premier League titles and three FA Cups.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He played youth soccer for Billingham Town.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data
