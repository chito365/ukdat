## Who is  Natasha Dupeyron
                  
                  
                  
Both a television actress and a pop vocalist, this Mexican entertainer starred on a youth telenovela called Peregrina and performed from 2011 until 2014 with a band called Eme 15. Additionally, she played the role of Natalia on a teen musical television series titled Miss XV.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
When she was less than three weeks old, she appeared on screen for the first time on a telenovela called Doctor Cándido Pérez.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data 
                  
                  
                  
She played a visually impaired teenager on the telenovela Juro Que Te Amo.
                  
                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Natasha Dupeyron
                  
                  
                  
She grew up in Mexico City as the daughter of actor Humberto Dupeyron. Her older siblings, Odin and Odette, also pursued acting careers.
                  
                
                
                
## People Related With  Natasha Dupeyron
                  
                  
                  
She and fellow Mexican actress Paulina Goto both performed with Eme 15 and co-starred on Miss XV; in addition, they were cast together on a telenovela called Las Mejores Amigas.
