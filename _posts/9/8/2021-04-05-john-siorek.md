## Who is  John Siorek
                  
                  
                  
Chicago based drummer and member of the popular pop punk band Knuckle Puck, who are best known for their songs &#8220;Disdain&#8221; and &#8220;No Good.&#8221; He has also played drums for the band William Bonney.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He and his fellow band members Joe Taylor and Kevin Maida started out doing cover songs in 2010. They eventually shifted to writing original songs with the addition of Nick Cassanto in 2011.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data 
                  
                  
                  
His band&#8217;s EP While I Stay Secluded peaked at #5 on the Heatseekers Albums chart.
                  
                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Siorek
                  
                  
                  
He was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois.
                  
                
                
                
## People Related With  John Siorek
                  
                  
                  
He and guitarist Kevin Maida both play in Knuckle Puck.
