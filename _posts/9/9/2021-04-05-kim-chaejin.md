## Who is  Kim Chaejin
                  
                  
                  
Main rapper and main dancer for the Korean dance girl group LAYSHA that debuted with a self-titled single. She specializes in jazz dance.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
She was previously part of a group called LXIA in 2012.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data
