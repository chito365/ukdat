## Who is  Kyle Fasel
                  
                  
                  
Bass guitarist who was a co-founder of the band Real Friends after he called lead guitarist Dave Knox. He also became the main lyricist for the group.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He was working on music before Real Friends but was not happy with it. Real Friends would form in 2010.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data 
                  
                  
                  
He authors spoken word poetry and wrote a poetry book called Nothing Memorable Stays the Same.
                  
                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kyle Fasel
                  
                  
                  
He is from Tinley Park, Illinois.
