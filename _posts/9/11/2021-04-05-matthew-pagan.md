## Who is  Matthew Pagan
                  
                  
                  
One-fourth of the English boy band Collabro that would go on to win the 8th series of Britain&#8217;s Got Talent in 2014. They released a debut album called Stars in 2014 that debuted at #1 in the UK.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He was working as a kitchen salesman at the time of his Britain&#8217;s Got Talent audition with Collabro.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data 
                  
                  
                  
Their album Stars also charted in the Netherlands, Ireland, New Zealand and Scotland, with a top 10 charting position for the latter three countries.
                  
                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Matthew Pagan
                  
                  
                  
He was raised in Carlisle, Cumbria, England.
                  
                
                
                
## People Related With  Matthew Pagan
                  
                  
                  
He has performed alongside Michael Auger, Jamie Lambert, Richard Hadfield and Thomas Redgrave in Collabro.
