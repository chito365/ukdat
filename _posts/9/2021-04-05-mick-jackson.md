## Who is  Mick Jackson
                  
                  
                  
Director of the Whitney Houston vehicle The Bodyguard and the biographical HBO film Temple Grandin.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He directed documentaries and dramas for BBC TV.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data
