## Who is  Michael Rooker
                  
                  
                  
Played zombie apocalypse survivor Merle Dixon on the television series The Walking Dead. Dixon was his first recurring role on television, though he has guest-starred on multiple shows since 1986. He also plays Yondu Udonta in the 2014 film Guardians of the Galaxy.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
He made his acting debut as the star of the 1986 film Henry: Portrait of a Serial Killer, in which he played Henry Lee Lucas.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data 
                  
                  
                  
He widened his career in the 2010 James Felix McKenney horror film Hypothermia.
                  
                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Michael Rooker
                  
                  
                  
He grew up sharing a house with his mother and eight brothers and sisters in Chicago. He had two daughters with his wife Margot Rooker.
                  
                
                
                
## People Related With  Michael Rooker
                  
                  
                  
He played a major role in the 2011 thriller Mysteria, starring Billy Zane.
