## Who is  Lee Si-yeon
                  
                  
                  
Main vocalist for the all-female K-pop group Dreamcatcher that was formed by Happy Face Entertainment on January 12, 2017. Her stage name is Siyeon.
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity 
                  
                  
                  
She was previously a member of the group MINX. She finished in 97th as a participant on the YG survival show MIXNINE.
                  
                
                
                
## Random data
