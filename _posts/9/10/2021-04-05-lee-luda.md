## Who is  Lee Luda
                  
                  
                  
Korean pop singer who became part of the 13-member K-pop girl group Cosmic Girls (aka WJSN), which was formed by Starship Entertainment. They became known for their debut mini album Would You Like?
                  
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
