## Who is Ralph Saenz
                  
                  
                  
Lead singer of the glam metal band Steel Panther who also performed with L.A. Guns and Nightfall, among others.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
                  
                  
                  
He sang for the Van Halen tribute band Atomic Punks from 1994 to 2008.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Random data
