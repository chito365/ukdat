## Who is Deepee
                  
                  
                  
English rapper best known as a member of the rap group Section Boyz. They released the mixtape Don&#8217;t Panic in September of 2015. The mixtape peaked at #36 on the UK Albums Chart and helped them win the MOBO Award for Best Newcomer.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
                  
                  
                  
He and the rest of the Section Boyz came together thanks to UK rapper Ghost who created the group. Their debut mixtape was titled Sectionly.
