## Who is Petey Williams
                  
                  
                  
Multiple-time X Division champion, former captain of Team Canada, and LLUSA Tag Team champion.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
                  
                  
                  
He spent four years working the independent wrestling circuit in Canada.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Random data
