## Who is Rashawn Ross
                  
                  
                  
Touring member of the Dave Matthews Band and well-known session musician.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
                  
                  
                  
He began studying at Berklee College of Music in 1996 and graduated in 2000.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Random data
