## Who is Big Boss Man
                  
                  
                  
Late professional wrestler remembered by wrestling fans as a four-time WWF Hardcore Champion and one-time WWF World Tag Team Champion. He died of a heart attack in his Georgia home at age 41. In 2016, he was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame.Â
