Television actor best known for playing Noah Lawson on the Australian soap opera Home and Away.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
                  
                  
                  
He made his first television appearance on the show Breakers while still attending high school.
                  
              
            
              
            
                
                
                
## Random data
