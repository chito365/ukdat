## Who is Hunter Schafer



Transgender woman who has worked as a model, actress, artist and activist for LGBT rights. She made her acting debut in the HBO series Euphoria in the role of Jules in 2019.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She protested against her birth state&#8217;s Public Facilities Privacy & Security Act, AKA the &#8220;bathroom bill,&#8221; as a high school student. In 2017, she graduated from the High School Visual Arts program at University of North Carolina School of the Arts.

                
                
                
## Random data



She was featured on Teen Vogue&#8217;s &#8220;21 Under 21&#8221; list. She has modeled for Dior, Tommy Hilfiger, Marc Jacobs and other luxury brands.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Hunter Schafer



She has two younger sisters and one younger brother. They were born to parents Katy Schafer and Mac Schafer.
