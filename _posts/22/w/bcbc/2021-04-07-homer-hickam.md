## Who is Homer Hickam



American author, military veteran, and one-time NASA engineer. He is best known for his New York Times bestselling memoir Rocket Boys. His other literary works include Torpedo Junction, Back to the Moon, and Sky of Stone.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



In 1964, he graduated from Virginia Tech, where he studied Industrial Engineering. In the early 1980s, he began working for NASA.

                
                
                
## Random data



He earned the Commendation Medal for his time spent serving in the Vietnam War.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Homer Hickam



He was born in Coalwood, West Virginia to Homer and Elsie Hickam. He married Linda Terry Hickam.
