## Who is HueningKai



Vocalist and maknae of the new BigHit Entertainment boys band TXT, who was the third member to be announced. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He is the first person not born in South Korea to debut under BigHit Entertainment. 

                
                
                
## Random data



TXT stands for Tomorrow X Together, which BigHit Entertainment has stated means &#8220;You and I are together in one dream and make tomorrow together.&#8221; 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of HueningKai



His father is Brazilian-German and his mother is Korean. His older sister is Lea from girls group VIVA. His younger sister is named Bahiye.

                
                
                
## People Related With HueningKai



Choi Yeon Jun is the TXT rapper.
