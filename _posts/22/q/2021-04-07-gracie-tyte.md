## Who is Gracie Tyte



Equestrian rider whose Instagram account, pony_nuts, follows the training journey of her horses, including Danny and Johnny. Her horse-related content has brought more than 160,000 followers to her account so far.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She got her first pony, Gyppo, at age 9.

                
                
                
## Random data
