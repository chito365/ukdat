## Who is Jon Eka Rock



One third of the Indonesian punk rock band Superman Is Dead. He serves as the group&#8217;s bassist and backing vocalist. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Superman Is Dead, or S.I.D., formed in 1995 and two years later released their debut indie album, Case 15. 

                
                
                
## Random data



In 2009, Superman Is Dead joined the Vans&#8217; Warped Tour and performed with From Bali With Rock. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jon Eka Rock



Like his bandmates, he is a native of Bali, Indonesia. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jon Eka Rock



The other members of Superman Is Dead include Bobby Bikul and Jerinx.
