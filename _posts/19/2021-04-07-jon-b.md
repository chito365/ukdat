## Who is Jon B



R&B singer who shot to stardom in the late &#8217;90s with platinum-selling albums like 1997&#8217;s Cool Relax. He is also a highly successful songwriter who has penned songs for artists like Michael Jackson and Toni Braxton.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was born in Providence, Rhode Island and first gained popularity with his debut solo album, Bonafide, in 1995.

                
                
                
## Random data



He received six Billboard Music Award nominations in 1998 for awards including Top R&B Artist and Top Hot R&B Single.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jon B



His full name is Jonathan David Buck. He married Danette Jackson in 2007 and has children named Azure and L&#8217;wren.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jon B



His 1997 single &#8220;Are U Still Down&#8221; features rapper Tupac Shakur.
