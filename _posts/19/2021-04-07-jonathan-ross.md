## Who is Jonathan Ross



British television personality who hosted The Jonathan Ross Show from 2011 to 2013. He also hosted Friday Night with Jonathan Ross.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He earned a degree in European History from the University of London&#8217;s School of Slavonic and East European Studies.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was also a longtime presenter on Film 72.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jonathan Ross



He got married to Jane Goldman in 1988 and the couple had three children: Betty, Harvey, and Jack.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jonathan Ross



He had a supporting acting role with Eddie Izzard in the 2001 TV movie We Know Where You Live.
