## Who is Joseph Armstrong



Drummer for the band SWMRS (Swimmers), previously known as Emily&#8217;s Army, who formed the band in 2004 with the Becker brothers. They released three studio albums between 2011 and 2016, including Drive North.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He and Cole Becker were inspired to play music after watching the movie School of Rock.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was featured on the drums for Matt Grocott & The Shrives&#8217; debut EP titled Turn Me On. He and bassist Seb Mueller performed in a band together called DDG.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joseph Armstrong



He has a younger brother named Jakob who is a guitarist and singer-songwriter. Jakob once filled in as a touring musician for SWMRS. Their parents are Adrienne Armstrong and Green Day guitarist Billie Joe Armstrong.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joseph Armstrong



He, Max Becker, and Cole Becker were the original members of SWMRS.
