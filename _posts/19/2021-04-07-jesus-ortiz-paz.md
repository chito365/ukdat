## Who is Jesús Ortiz-Paz



Musician popular in the Latino community who gained fame as part of the group Fuerza Regida. He uploaded some of the group&#8217;s songs to his personal YouTube channel, Jesus Ortiz. They included &#8220;Discuple Usted&#8221; and &#8220;El Manuelito,&#8221; among others.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He and his band created their official YouTube channel in 2017.

                
                
                
## Random data
