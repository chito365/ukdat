## Who is Jo Anne Worley



Boisterous performer who was featured on Rowan and Martin&#8217;s Laugh-In. Her voice acting credits include A Goofy Movie and Beauty and the Beast.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



In 1965, she started her own nightclub act in Greenwich Village, which led her to become discovered by actor Merv Griffin.

                
                
                
## Random data



Her first musical role was in a 1961 production of Wonderful Town.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jo Anne Worley



She was married to Roger Perry from 1975 until 2000.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jo Anne Worley



She played a role in The Wizard of Oz, starring Judy Garland.
