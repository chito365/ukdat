## Who is Jerry Jones



Businessman who started an oil business in Arkansas called Jones Oil and Land Lease that generated huge profits. In 1989, he became owner of the NFL&#8217;s Dallas Cowboys and developed a reputation as one of the league&#8217;s most flashy and wealthy owners.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was an offensive lineman for the University of Arkansas in 1964 and won a national championship with the team.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was dubbed the least favorite sports personality in three states by a 2003 Sports Illustrated poll. In 2017, he was elected to the Pro Football Hall of Fame.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jerry Jones



He raised three children, Charlotte, Stephen and Jerry Jr., with his wife Gene Jones. He and Gene also have nine grandchildren.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jerry Jones



He clashed with former Cowboys coach Jimmy Johnson, leading to Johnson&#8217;s departure from the team, which was documented in 2008&#8217;s Playing to Win.
