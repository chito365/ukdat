## Who is Joey Votto



All-Star MLB first baseman who was named National League MVP in 2010 with the Cincinnati Reds after hitting 37 home runs, recording 113 RBIs and batting .324.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He played both basketball and hockey in addition to baseball in high school.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was named to his first MLB All-Star team in 2010.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joey Votto



He was raised with twin brothers named Paul and Ryan.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joey Votto



He joined second baseman Brandon Phillips on the Reds in 2007.
