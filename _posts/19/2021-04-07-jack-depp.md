## Who is Jack Depp



Born Jack John Christopher Depp III, he is the son of Johnny Depp and pop singer Vanessa Paradis. He appeared in the 2016 movie Yoga Hosers. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was born in Neuilly-sur-Seine, Nanterre, France.

                
                
                
## Random data



He spent most of his time in the US following his father&#8217;s split from his mother.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jack Depp



His older sister is Lily-Rose Melody Depp. His grandparents are Betty Sue Palmer, André Paradis, Corinne Paradis and John Christopher Depp.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jack Depp
