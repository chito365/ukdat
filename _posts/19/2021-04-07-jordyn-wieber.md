## Who is Jordyn Wieber



Olympic gymnast who helped the U.S. win Team Gold at the 2012 London Games and became the World Women&#8217;s All-Around Champion in 2011. She announced her retirement from gymnastics in 2015. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She began learning gymnastics at age three, but stopped to take dance classes. She joined the Silverstars program, and qualified for the Level 10 Junior Olympic program in 2005.

                
                
                
## Random data



She took home the Team and All-Around gold medals at the 2011 Tokyo World Championships.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jordyn Wieber



Her parents, Rita and Dave, raised her along with her three siblings, Lindsay, Ryan, and Kyra. She began dating fellow Olympic gymnast Chris Brooks in 2017. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jordyn Wieber



She shared the 2012 Team Gold with Gabby Douglas at the 2012 Olympics.
