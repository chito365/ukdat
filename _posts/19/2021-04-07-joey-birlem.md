## Who is Joey Birlem



Popular Instagrammer who supported Jacob Sartorius on the Sartorian Summer Send-Off tour. His Instagram following is north of 1.4 million and he has over 2.1 million fans on TikTok. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He grew up a skateboarder. He first grew his social following on TikTok. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He and his friend Tayler Holder created a collaborative YouTube channel called Joey and Taylor in January of 2016. He also has a self-titled YouTube channel where he has posted vlogs, challenges and other videos. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joey Birlem



He is from Los Angeles, California. He has two sisters, Sophia and Bella, and brothers named Scotty and Kashius. His mother&#8217;s name is Jen Valdez.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joey Birlem
