## Who is Jimmy Choo



Malaysian fashion designer who founded Jimmy Choo Ltd., which is known for its selection of handmade women&#8217;s shoes.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He made shoes for the first time when he was 11 years old and attended Cordwainers Technical College in Hackney, England.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has won numerous awards including the 2011 Design for Asia Award in the World&#8217;s Outstanding Chinese Designer category.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jimmy Choo



He married Rebecca Choo. His son Danny Choo became a popular blogger.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jimmy Choo



He founded Jimmy Choo Ltd with British Vogue magazine editor Tamara Mellon in 1996.
