## Who is Joe Alwyn



British actor who broke through with his starring role as Billy from the Ang Lee-directed film Billy Lynn&#8217;s Long Halftime Walk. Other film credits include The Sense of an Ending, The Favourite and Mary Queen of Scots. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He attended the National Youth Theatre and read English and drama at the University of Bristol. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He graduated with a Bachelor of Arts Honors degree from the Royal Central School of Speech & Drama. He was cast in Billy Lynn&#8217;s Long Halftime Walk while in his final year of school.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joe Alwyn



His mom worked in psychotherapy and his dad was a documentary filmmaker. He began dating Taylor Swift in 2016.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joe Alwyn



He starred alonngside Kristen Stewart in Billy Lynn&#8217;s Long Halftime Walk.
