## Who is Jessica Rose Shears



Published glamour model who has traveled the world, including photo shoots for Zoo and Elite Online. She has been represented by BMA Models and GIRL Management. She was later cast as a reality personality on the ITV2 dating show Love Island.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She began modeling in about 2012.

                
                
                
## Random data



She became an Instagram phenomenon with more than 1.2 million followers. She has published lifestyle, travel and beauty vlogs on her Jessica Rose UK YouTube channel.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jessica Rose Shears



She is from Devon, England and she has a sister named Emily. She began dating her co-star Dominic Lever in 2017 and they married in 2018. In October 2019, she gave birth to their first child.
