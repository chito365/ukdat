## Who is Jonny Gray



Nickelodeon actor who shot to fame thanks to his starring role on the 2014 television series Max and Shred in the title role of Max Asher. He also plays Josh Luders on the TV series Ride. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He made his professional acting debut in the 2013 television documentary series Paranormal Witness. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He appeared in multiple episodes of Annedroids in the role of Zack. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jonny Gray



He is from Ontario, Canada and has two older brothers. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jonny Gray
