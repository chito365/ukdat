## Who is Joey Zehr



Former drummer for the Boston-based power pop band The Click Five. He also starred alongside his band members in the movie Taking Five. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was a double major in production/engineering and business at the Berklee College of Music. He played in various local bands that were unsuccessful.

                
                
                
## Random data



When The Click Five were just beginning, he and the group would perform under different band names to get around local club rules from preventing artists playing shows too close to one another.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joey Zehr



He got married to Kate Albrecht from the Mr. Kate YouTube channel; he has frequently appeared there. He and Kate welcomed a son named Moon together in June 2019. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Joey Zehr



He was friends with fellow The Click Five band member Eric Dill in high school.
