## Who is Jesse Rutherford



Lead singer for the California indie band The Neighbourhood. Their hit single &#8220;Sweater Weather&#8221; reached number one on Billboard&#8217;s alternative chart in June 2013. He is joined in The Neighbourhood by guitarists Jeremy Freedman and Zach Abels, bassist Mikey Margott and drummer Brandon Fried. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He acted when he was younger, playing Daniel on The Young and the Restless.  

                
                
                
## Random data



He also appeared in such productions as Life or Something Like It, Ted Bundy and Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jesse Rutherford



His was raised in Newberry Park, California. In 2015 he began dating Devon Lee Carlson whom he was friends with prior to their romantic relationship. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jesse Rutherford



He impersonated Elvis Presley and NSYNC in talent shows.
