## Who is John Lasseter



Animator and director of several Pixar films including Toy Story, A Bug&#8217;s Life and Cars. In 1988, his film Tin Toy received an Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film and in 1996, Toy Story received a Special Achievement Award from the Academy Awards.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He began his collegiate studies at Pepperdine University, then transferred to the California Institute of the Arts to study animation.
