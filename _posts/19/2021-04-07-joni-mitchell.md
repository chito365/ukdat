## Who is Joni Mitchell



Folk singer-songwriter whose album Blue, which features songs like &#8220;A Case of You&#8221; and &#8220;River,&#8221; is considered one of the greatest albums of all time by Rolling Stone.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She grew up without indoor plumbing or running water. Her songs like &#8220;Chelsea Morning&#8221; were covered by U.S. folk singers, giving Mitchell a foothold in the music industry.

                
                
                
## Random data



One of her most famous songs is &#8220;Big Yellow Taxi.&#8221;

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joni Mitchell



She got married to Chuck Mitchell in 1965; after their separation in 1966, she married Larry Klein on November 21, 1982.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joni Mitchell



She has collaborated with jazz legend Herbie Hancock.
