## Who is Joe Diffie



Country singer and songwriter who earned five #1 singles, including &#8220;Third Rock from The Sun&#8221; and &#8220;Home.&#8221;

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He played football, baseball, golf, and track in high school. He worked in a foundry while playing music on the side.

                
                
                
## Random data



He won a 1998 Grammy Award for Best Country Collaboration with &#8220;Same Old Train.&#8221;

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joe Diffie



He married his third wife, Theresa Crump, in 2000; they had five children together named Parker, Kara, Drew, Tyler, and Kylie.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joe Diffie



He was a featured artist on Mary Carpenter&#8217;s single &#8220;Not Too Much to Ask.&#8221;
