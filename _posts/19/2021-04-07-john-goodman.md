## Who is John Goodman



Large-as-life actor who played husband Dan Conner of TV&#8217;s Roseanne from 1988 to 1997. The role earned him a Golden Globe Award for Best Actor in 1993. He also played roles in Monsters, Inc., The Big Lebowski, and O Brother, Where Art Thou?

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He played basketball and football in high school, earning a college scholarship for football.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has hosted Saturday Night Live several times and often worked with the Cohen brothers.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Goodman



He got married to Anna Beth Hartzog in October 1989, and the couple had their first daughter in 1990.

                
                
                
## People Related With John Goodman



He was in the Broadway production Loose Ends with Bruce Willis.
