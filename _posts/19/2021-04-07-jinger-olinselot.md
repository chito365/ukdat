## Who is Jinger Olinselot



Otherwise known as Jingerrific, she shares a YouTube following of more than 3.1 million subscribers with her husband Carl Olinselot. Together they run the joint family channel Carl & Jinger, formerly called phamdamily, documenting their lives with their three sons.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Before the success of the Carl & Jinger channel, she earned a degree in human resources from Idaho State University and later worked as a manager for the Idaho Central Credit Union.

                
                
                
## Random data



Jinger runs a second YouTube channel called Jingerrific, which she has transformed into an outlet for beauty tutorials and craft videos.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jinger Olinselot



She and Carl gave birth to three boys named Gage, Luke, and Kyle.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jinger Olinselot



She and Carl are friends with a vast array of YouTube stars, including fellow family vloggers April Orgill and her husband Davey.
