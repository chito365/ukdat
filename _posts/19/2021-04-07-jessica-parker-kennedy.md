## Who is Jessica Parker Kennedy



Canadian actress best known for her role on the television series Smallville. She played Max on Black Sails.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She played an elf in the 2006 television movie Santa Baby.

                
                
                
## Random data



She was J.J. Abrams&#8217; first choice for the role of Lizzy in the spy series Undercovers, but she was replaced by another actress following the show&#8217;s pilot episode.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jessica Parker Kennedy



She was born in Calgary, Alberta, to a family of African, Italian, and Russian descent.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jessica Parker Kennedy



She and Selena Gomez were both in the cast of the 2008 film Another Cinderella Story.
