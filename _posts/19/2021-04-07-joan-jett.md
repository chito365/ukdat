## Who is Joan Jett



She rose to fame as the lead singer of Joan Jett & the Blackhearts and became considered the Queen of Rock &#8216;n&#8217; Roll for hits such as &#8220;I Love Rock &#8216;N&#8217; Roll.&#8221;

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Born Joan Marie Larkin, she quit guitar lessons when she was 14 years old because she did not want to learn folk songs.

                
                
                
## Random data



Her band recorded a cover of &#8220;Crimson and Clover&#8221; and gained fame for their 1980 debut album, Bad Reputation.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joan Jett



Her family moved to West Covina, California. She took her mother&#8217;s maiden name, Jett, after her parents divorced.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joan Jett



She toured with Steven Tyler during Aerosmith&#8217;s 2007 World Tour.
