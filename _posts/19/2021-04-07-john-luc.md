## Who is John Luc



Vietnamese-Chinese YouTuber known as mychonny who runs 5 different channels. He creates what he calls &#8220;Asianese&#8221; videos of himself, friends, and family. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He started his YouTube career in March of 2008, at first struggling to realize if YouTube was the path he wanted to travel down. 

                
                
                
## Random data



All five of his channels, mychonny, yourchonny, chonnyday, chonnygame, and kpopchonny, are extremely popular. Mychonny, with over 1 million subscribers, is the most popular. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Luc



He resides in Melbourne, Australia. His sister Serinna is four years older than he is. 

                
                
                
## People Related With John Luc



He published a popular parody cover of PSY&#8217;s &#8220;Gangnam Style&#8221; to his kpopchonny channel.
