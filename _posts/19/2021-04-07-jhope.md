## Who is JHope



Born Jung Hoseok, he is a member of Kpop group Bangtan Boys aka BTS. In 2014, his band released an album titled Wake Up and a recording titled Dark & Wild. In 2017, they became the first Kpop group to win a Billboard award. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He joined the other six members of the group to debut in June of 2013, with their first title song &#8220;No More Dream.&#8221;  

                
                
                
## Random data



He and the Bangtan Boys were named New Artist of the Year at the 2013 MelOn Music Awards, 2013 Golden Disk Awards, 2014 Seoul Music Awards, and 2014 Gaon Chart K-Pop Awards. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of JHope



He has a sister named Jung Da-won who has grown popular on Instagram.

                
                
                
## People Related With JHope



He is joined by Kim Taehyung in the group Bangtan Boys.
