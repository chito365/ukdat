## Who is Joey Essex



Star of the popular British reality series The Only Way Is Essex. He also became known for his singing career and his chart-topping single &#8220;Reem.&#8221; 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Prior to appearing on the show in 2011, he was working on a music career. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He started his own shop, Fusey, in Brentwood. In 2013, he competed on the reality series Splash! 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joey Essex



His mother Tina passed away when he was just ten years old. His sister Frankie and his cousin Chloe both appeared on The Only Way is Essex. He had an on again, off again relationship with model Sam Faiers. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Joey Essex



Mark Wright, the former footballer, co-starred with him on The Only Way Is Essex.
