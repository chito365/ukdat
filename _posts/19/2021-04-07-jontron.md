## Who is JonTron



Known by his internet handle JonTron, his YouTube videos consist of comedically reviewed gameplay of poorly received games. He also co-founded the website Normal Boots.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He first joined the YouTube world in 2010, debuting with a review of the game Daikatana on JonTronShow.

                
                
                
## Random data



He hosted the webseries Game Grumps, which he created with Arin Hanson.
