## Who is Jillian Webber



Younger sister of Vine phenomenon Jake Webber who has been seen in Jake&#8217;s videos. She also became popular herself on the lip sync app TikTok, earning over 480,000 fans on her jillsta account. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was just eleven years old when her brother Jake began using Vine. She and Jake both posted their first Vines in 2013.
