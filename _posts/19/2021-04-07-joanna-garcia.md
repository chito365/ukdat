## Who is Joanna Garcia



Screen actress best known for her role as Cheyenne Hart Montgomery on the hit sitcom Reba. She also played Megan Smith on the series Privileged.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was discovered by Nickelodeon while she was attending high school. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She had a recurring role on the TV show Are You Afraid of the Dark?

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joanna Garcia



She married Yankees outfielder Nick Swisher in December 2010. They had their first child, a daughter named Emerson, on May 21, 2013 and a second daughter, Sailor Stevie, in June of 2016.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joanna Garcia



She had a four episode arc on the popular show Gossip Girl as the love interest of Chace Crawford&#8217;s character.
