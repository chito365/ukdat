## Who is Jamie Farr



Played the role of cross-dressing Corporal Klinger on TV&#8217;s M*A*S*H and became a regular on The Red Skelton Show in the 1950s.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



His first role won him two dollars in a local acting contest at age 11.

                
                
                
## Random data
