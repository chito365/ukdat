## Who is Jodelle Ferland



She played the role Bree Tanner in the Twilight Saga and appeared in Case 39. In 2006, she received widespread attention for her performance in the horror film Silent Hill. She was later cast in the television series Dark Matter.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She began acting in commercials at the age of two and made her film debut when she was four.

                
                
                
## Random data



She provided voices for characters in the videogame BioShock 2.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jodelle Ferland



Her parents are Valerie and Marc Ferland. Her sister, Marisha, is an actor and her brother, Jeremy, is a musician.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jodelle Ferland



She costarred with Renee Zellweger in the 2009 horror film Case 39.
