## Who is Johnny Tannerites



Patriarch of the YouTube vlogging channel Tannerites which features his family of nine. The channel has earned more than 2.4 million subscribers. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He and his wife Sarah Tannerites created the Tannerites channel in July of 2015, uploading a first video called &#8220;TANNERITES FIRST VIDEO! First day of School, last day of Radiation!!&#8221;

                
                
                
## Random data



His family entered into CVX Live&#8217;s Next Big YouTuber Contest in 2016.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Johnny Tannerites



He and Sarah have children named Branson, Allie, Daniell, Savannah, Lizzy, Az and Canyon.

                
                
                
## People Related With Johnny Tannerites



He and Jesse of the SmellyBellyTV YouTube channel are both patriarchs for their respective family vlog channels.
