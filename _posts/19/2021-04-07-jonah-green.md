## Who is Jonah Green



Popular video content creator on both YouTube and Vine who is the co-owner of Mutantboyfriend Productions alongside his partner Sam Katz. Together they create skits on YouTube and completed a film called Insight. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He and Sam created their Mutantboyfriend channel on March 16, 2009. Jonah posted the first video to the channel on July 30, 2009. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He became one of the seven YouTubers on the channel Settle Down Kids. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jonah Green



He has a younger sister named Shoshana. He dated YouTuber Claudia Sulewski. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jonah Green



He and Ryan Abe are a part of the YouTube channel Settle Down Kids. He has collaborated with fellow YouTubers Arden Rose, Maddi Bragg and Oli White.
