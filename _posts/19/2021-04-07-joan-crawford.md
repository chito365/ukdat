## Who is Joan Crawford



Glamorous prima donna who won an Academy Award for her role in Mildred Pierce. She starred in many rags-to-riches movies as a flapper during the Great Depression.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was originally spotted while dancing in Detroit.

                
                
                
## Random data



In addition to being a flapper in the late &#8217;20s, she was on the board of directors for Pepsi-Cola.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joan Crawford



She married four times and divorced three times. Her last husband, Alfred Steele, died in 1959.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joan Crawford



She starred with Bette Davis in the 1962 movie Whatever Happened to Baby Jane?
