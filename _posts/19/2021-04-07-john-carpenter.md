## Who is John Carpenter



American director and composer known for his horror, thriller and science fiction films. His filmography includes The Thing, Big Trouble in Little China, Halloween, Escape from New York and Starman. He has composed most of the music for his films. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He made short horror films on 8mm before high school. He was enrolled at the University of Southern California&#8217;s School of Cinematic Arts, but dropped out.

                
                
                
## Random data



His film Halloween was selected for preservation in the National Film Library in 2006 by the United States Library of Congress.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Carpenter



He met actress Adrienne Barbeau on the set of his television movie Somebody&#8217;s Watching Me, and the two were married on January 1, 1979. They divorced five years later. In 1990, Carpenter married Sandy King. He has one son. 

                
                
                
## People Related With John Carpenter



He was greatly influenced by the works of director Alfred Hitchcock.
