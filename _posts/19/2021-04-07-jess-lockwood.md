## Who is Jess Lockwood



Professional bull rider who ranked as the #1 bull rider in the world in 2017 after finishing his 2016 campaign with a Rookie of the Year honor.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He grew up around a rodeo atmosphere. In 2013, he won the Class B-C 98-pound Montana state wrestling championship.

                
                
                
## Random data



He rang the opening bell at the New York Stock Exchange.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jess Lockwood



He is from Volborg, Montana. His father Ed is a former saddle bronc champion and his mom Angie was a barrel racer. He has a brother named Jake. He married equestrian rider Hailey Kinsel in 2019. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jess Lockwood



In 2017, while he sat at #1 in the world ranking, JB Mauney opened the year ranked in the top 25 worldwide.
