## Who is Jonna Jinton



YouTuber who is known for being an artist, photographer, and singer known for her kulning style. She posts vlogs and videos of her singing to her self-titled YouTube channel which has over 2.1 million subscribers. She also has a large following on Instagram. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She first began sharing her photos to Instagram in June of 2012. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She runs a Patreon page where fans can donate to help her music career. She offers a variety of services including tutorials on singing, personal chats to discuss music and her career, and a thank you credit on her upcoming album.
