## Who is Jersey McClure



Skyrocketed to fame as the younger brother of the McClure Twins. He and his family star on the McClure Family YouTube channel.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity

## Random data



His parents run an Instagram account for him, jerseytayomcclure. The account has more than 220,000 followers. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jersey McClure



His father, Justin, is a comedian and his mother, Ami, is an Instagrammer. He has two older sisters, Ava and Alexis, who make up the McClure Twins.
