## Who is Jonathan Cheban



Founder of the public relations firm CommandPR who gained fame for his connections to the Kardashians.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He worked in public relations after graduating from Hofstra University in 1995.

                
                
                
## Random data



He appeared in episodes of Kourtney and Kim Take New York and Kourtney and Kim Take Miami.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jonathan Cheban



His father Mikhail was a diamond dealer and his mother Galina was a real estate agent.
