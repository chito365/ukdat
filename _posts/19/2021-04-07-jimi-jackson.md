## Who is Jimi Jackson



Actor, comedian and filmmaker popular on YouTube with more than 1.1 million subscribers, and even more relevant on Facebook where he had more than 900,000 page likes. He is known for poking fun at Kiwi culture.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He got started online by uploading Vines to his YouTube, Facebook and Instagram accounts towards the beginning of 2013.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has often collaborated with the Maori dance crew Jgeeks. He and Shae Sterling created a kickstarter page to fund an alien encounter movie starring Jackson and directed by Sterling.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jimi Jackson



He is from Auckland, New Zealand. He has featured his younger brother, Brandon, in his videos. His sister has also appeared on his YouTube channel. He has a son named Cairo and a daughter named Zaria with his fiancee Lana, whom he proposed to in 2018. They have been dating since 2013.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jimi Jackson



He has commented on the views of Percy Inglis on his YouTube channel.
