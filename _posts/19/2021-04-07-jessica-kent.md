## Who is Jessica Kent



YouTuber who uses her self-titled channel mostly as a place to share her experiences as a recovering methamphetamine and heroin addict. Her experiences with addiction, prison, and her subsequent reassimilation into civilian life are shared with 430,000 subscribers. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She has lived in Chicago, Illinois. 

                
                
                
## Random data



Much of her channel is about educating her viewers about the prison experience, as seen in videos like &#8220;Answering Your Assumptions About Female Prisons.&#8221; 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jessica Kent



She gave birth to her first daughter while in prison. She got engaged to her fiancé Reece in December 2020. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jessica Kent
