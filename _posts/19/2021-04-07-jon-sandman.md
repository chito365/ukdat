## Who is Jon Sandman



Online gamer who is known for his self-titled Twitch live streaming channel. He has gained popularity there for his humorous commentary over his gameplay, as well as frequent interaction with his viewers.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He began streaming on Twitch as a hobby. In August 2014, he became a full-time streamer.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has garnered more than 420,000 followers to his Twitch channel. He has also operated his own self-titled YouTube channel, where he frequently posts highlights from his live streams, and has amassed more than 1.2 million subscribers there.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jon Sandman



He was born and raised in California. In September 2017, he and his wife welcomed a son named Bryce.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jon Sandman



He and fellow online gamer Robert Latsky both rose to prominence due to their sizable Twitch live streaming channels.
