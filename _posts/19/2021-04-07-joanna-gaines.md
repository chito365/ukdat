## Who is Joanna Gaines



HGTV personality on Fixer Upper who stars alongside her husband Chip Gaines. She and Chip own and operate Magnolia Homes, a remodeling and design business, and she is also the lead designer for the company. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She earned a degree in communications from Baylor University before heading to New York as an intern. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She opened a boutique of her own in 2003. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joanna Gaines



She was born in Kansas and raised in Texas. She and her husband have 5 kids together named Drake, Ella, Duke, Emmie and Crew. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Joanna Gaines



She joined Sarah Richardson as a HGTV lifestyle host for the network.
