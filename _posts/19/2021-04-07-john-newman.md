## Who is John Newman



Soul and pop singer best known for his chart topping single &#8220;Love Me Again,&#8221; which was released as part of his debut album Tribute. He was nominated for Best British Male at the 2014 BRIT Awards.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



When he was 20, he moved to London, where he started his first band and was subsequently signed by Island Records.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was featured on two Rudimental songs including &#8220;Not Giving In&#8221; and &#8220;Feel the Love,&#8221; the latter of which peaked at #1 on the UK Singles Chart.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Newman



He grew up with his older brother James and single mother after his alcoholic father left the family when he was six.

                
                
                
## People Related With John Newman



In 2013, he appeared on Jools Holland&#8217;s Hottenanny and Holland&#8217;s Rhythm and Blues Orchestra.
