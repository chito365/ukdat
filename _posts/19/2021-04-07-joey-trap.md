## Who is Joey Trap



Also referring to himself as Kloudgod, he is a hip hop artist with a couple of 2017 projects titled Free Trap, Trap Jack 2 and Misadventures of Trap Jack. One of his top tracks is titled &#8220;I Got Top At Bikini Bottom.&#8221; He later founded the music group Kloud Gang.
