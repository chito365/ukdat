## Who is Jesse Metcalfe



Actor best known for his role on ABC&#8217;s Desperate Housewives. He later starred in the TNT revival of the show Dallas.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He made his acting debut on the successful soap opera, Passions.

                
                
                
## Random data

## Family & Everyday Life of Jesse Metcalfe
