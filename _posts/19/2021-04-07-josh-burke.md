## Who is Josh Burke



Best recognized as the lead guitarist for the Florida-born punk band The Red Jumpsuit Apparatus. Joining the band in 2011, he has since worked on their EP Et Tu, Brute and the album simply titled 4.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Having been a fan of The Red Jumpsuit Apparatus for many years previously, after former guitarists Duke Kitchens and Matt Carter stepped down, he became the lead guitarist for the band in July of 2011.

                
                
                
## Random data



Outside music, he is a fan of mixed martial arts.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Josh Burke



He has a sister named Brooke and often features photos of her and his parents on his Instagram account.

                
                
                
## People Related With Josh Burke



He is joined in The Red Jumpsuit Apparatus by brothers Ronnie and Randy Winter, both singers and guitarists.
