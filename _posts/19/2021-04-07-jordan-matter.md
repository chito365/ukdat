## Who is Jordan Matter



American photographer who won widespread acclaim for his New York Times bestselling book, Dancers Among Us. He followed up the book&#8217;s success with another bestseller in 2018, Born To Dance. He has over 6 million subscribers on his self-titled YouTube channel where he primarily posts dance videos. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He played baseball in high school and college.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has been profiled by major media outlets like NBC, CBS and the BBC.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jordan Matter



He is the grandson of notable photographer Herbert Matter. His parents are film director Alex Matter and model Paula Feiten. He has two children: Salish and Hudson, the latter of whom is also a photographer. Sofie Dossi taught his daughter contortion in one of his YouTube videos. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jordan Matter



He was greatly inspired by French photographer Henri Cartier-Bresson.
