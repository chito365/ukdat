## Who is Boef
                  
                  
                  
Sofiane Boussaadia is the French-born Dutch rapper Boef. His debut album, Slaaptekort, released in 2017, peaked at #1 on the Dutch Top 100 chart and on #4 on the German Top 200 chart.
