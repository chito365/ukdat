## Who is Indiana Massara



Pop singer who released her hit single &#8220;Drama&#8221; in July 2017 on JJJ. She joined the cast of the popular web series Chicken Girls as Rooney in 2017. Her first on screen performance was in the 2015 short Crafty: Or (The Unexpected Virtue of the Girl in Charge of Snacks).

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She started modelling after she got rejected by a few acting auditions due to her height. Her first runway casting was for LA Fashion Week when she was 13.
