## Who is Iker Casillas



One of the most accomplished Spanish goalkeepers in history, known for his long stints with Real Madrid and Spain&#8217;s national team; helped lead the Spanish national team to its first ever World Cup Championship in 2010. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He began playing for Real Madrid&#8217;s youth squad at the age of 9. 

                
                
                
## Random data



Real Madrid took 5 La Liga titles with Casillas in net between 2000 and 2012.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Iker Casillas



He was raised with one brother, Unai Casillas Fernandez. He began dating Sara Carbonero and the couple welcomed a son named Martin.

                
                
                
## People Related With Iker Casillas
