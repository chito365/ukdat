## Who is Gerard Way



Founding member of the American rock band My Chemical Romance, whose 2006 album, The Black Parade, went platinum and featured songs such as &#8220;Famous Last Words.&#8221; In 2014 he signed as a solo artist with Warner Bros and released the debut song &#8220;Action Cat.&#8221; 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He played the role of Peter Pan in his elementary school play and pursued a career as a comic book writer. He got a degree in fine arts from New York City&#8217;s School of Visual Arts. 

                
                
                
## Random data



Aside from his rock career, he also wrote the Eisner Award-winning comic book The Umbrella Academy. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Gerard Way



He married Lindsey Ann Ballato, bassist of the band Mindless Self Indulgence, in September 2007. In 2009, they had a daughter named Bandit. He is joined by his brother Mikey Way in My Chemical Romance. He is cousins with UFC commentator and podcaster Joe Rogan. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Gerard Way



He is also joined in My Chemical Romance by Frank Iero, Bob Bryar, and Ray Toro.
