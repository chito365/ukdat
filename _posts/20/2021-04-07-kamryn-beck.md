## Who is Kamryn Beck



Abby Lee Dance Company team member who joined the show Dance Moms during its 4th season. She previously performed at Krystie&#8217;s Dance Academy, Studio Bleu, and Pulse Dance Studio.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She started dancing when she was 3 years old. She first appeared on Dance Moms in the episode &#8220;Guess Who&#8217;s Back?&#8221; 

                
                
                
## Random data



She won the title of Junior Miss NexStar at the How Do You Like Them Apples? Akron competition and won first place in the junior category at the NYCDA competition. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kamryn Beck



She is from Ohio and later relocated to Pennsylvania.

                
                
                
## People Related With Kamryn Beck



She is good friends with fellow Dance Moms star Kaeli Ware.
