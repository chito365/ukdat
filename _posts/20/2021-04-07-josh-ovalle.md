## Who is Josh Ovalle



Award-winning filmmaker selected in over 50 festivals and known for his Vine videos. He has promoted for MTV at events and shows, including live from the 2015 MTV Movie Awards hosted by Amy Schumer. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Baking was essentially his only hobby before he became a filmmaker. When he was 13, he created a film called Minimum Max that was viewed over half a million times, featured in the Huffington Post, and selected to 25 film festivals.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was discovered by MTV and the YouTube community when he was 15 years old following the release of his film Two and a Quarter, which was selected to more than 50 festivals, including the Toronto International Film Festival.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Josh Ovalle



He has brothers named Lucas and Elijah.
