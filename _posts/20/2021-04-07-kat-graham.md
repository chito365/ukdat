## Who is Kat Graham



Teen Choice Award-winning actress who played Bonnie Bennett on the hit CW television series The Vampire Diaries. She played the character Jamie in the 2009 comedy film 17 Again. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She entered the entertainment business through commercials and made her first film appearance in the 1998 film The Parent Trap. 

                
                
                
## Random data



Her music career skyrocketed after being featured in Will.I.Am&#8217;s song &#8220;I Got It From My Mama.&#8221; She also modeled for the Mary Kay company in 2002.  

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kat Graham



She was raised in Los Angeles. Her father Joseph was a music executive. She was once engaged to Cottrell Guidry but the couple split before they wed. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Kat Graham



She had her first television role in the hit Disney series Lizzie McGuire, starring Hilary Duff.
