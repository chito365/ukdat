## Who is K Michelle



R&B singer who charted singles with &#8220;Fakin&#8217; It&#8221; and &#8220;Can&#8217;t Do This.&#8221; She released her debut album in 2013 called Rebellious Soul.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She attended Florida A&M University on a music scholarship. She signed her first record contract with Jive Records in 2008.

                
                
                
## Random data



She was also a star on the reality series Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of K Michelle



In 2004, she welcomed a son. In 2016, she began dating Kastan Sims. She previously dated NBA shooting guard J.R. Smith. 

                
                
                
## People Related With K Michelle



Her 2012 single &#8220;Baby You&#8221; featured R. Kelly.
