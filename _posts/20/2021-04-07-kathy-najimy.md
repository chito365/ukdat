## Who is Kathy Najimy



Actress who provided the voice of Peggy Hill from the animated comedy King of the Hill and played Olive in NBC&#8217;s sitcom Veronica&#8217;s Closet. She appeared in the 1993 Disney feature Hocus Pocus and in the 2015 Disney TV movie Descendants. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Her breakout role was as sister Mary Patrick in the comedy film Sister Act &#8211; a role she reprised for the sequel. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She appeared on many celebrity editions of game shows, raising funds for gay and lesbian causes. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kathy Najimy



She had a daughter with her husband, actor and comedian Dan Finnerty. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Kathy Najimy



She appeared opposite America Ferrera in several episodes of the comedy series Ugly Betty.
