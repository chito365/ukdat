## Who is Josh Pieters



Social media star best known for his self-titled YouTube channel and for being a member of the Caspar Lee merchandise team. He has earned over 380,000 followers on Instagram. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was a right handed batsman and left-arm fast bowler for the South Western Districts Under-19s cricket team.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has amassed over 1.4 million subscribers to his self-titled YouTube channel, which he launched in November 2015.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Josh Pieters



He is from Knysna, Western Cape, South Africa and would later reside in London, England. He posted a tribute video to his mother with the February 2016 video &#8220;MY FAVOURITE PERSON IN THE WORLD.&#8221;
