## Who is Kayla Bylon



Instagram star and freelance model known for being a social media ambassador for several different brands and companies including Twenty 1 Rich, Champion, and O Mighty apparel. She shares her modeling shots and other lifestyle content with her over 650,000 Instagram followers. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She first began uploading images to her Instagram in October of 2017. She also posted her first video to her YouTube channel on December 8th, 2017. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She is known for having a sponsorship through Sweatcoin and promotes their business through her Instagram stories. She also runs a YouTube channel for her extended video content where she has over 190,000 subscribers.
