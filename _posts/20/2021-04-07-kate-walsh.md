## Who is Kate Walsh



Played Dr. Addison Montgomery on the television series Grey&#8217;s Anatomy and Private Practice. She also appeared in the 2012 film The Perks of Being a Wallflower and she plays Mrs. Baker on the Netflix series 13 Reasons Why. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She participated in theater while attending the University of Arizona. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She played Drew Carey&#8217;s love interest on The Drew Carey Show, and she appeared on several episodes of Conan O&#8217;Brien&#8217;s late night talk show. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kate Walsh



She got married to Alex Young on September 1, 2007, and they divorced on February 5, 2010. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Kate Walsh
