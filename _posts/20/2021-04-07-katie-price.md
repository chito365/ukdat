## Who is Katie Price



Tabloid fixture and glamour model previously known as Jordan who shot to fame after a topless spread of her ran in the British newspaper The Sun. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She had interests in swimming and horseback riding as a child. 

                
                
                
## Random data



Like Kim Kardashian, she parlayed an adult entertainment film into mega success, and was worth an estimated £45 million in 2012.

She has two ex-husbands, singer Peter Andre and fighter Alex Reid, and she married Kieran Hayler in 2013. She has three sons named Jett, Junior and Harvey and two daughters named Bunny and Princess Tiaamii. She began dating Carl Woods in 2020.
