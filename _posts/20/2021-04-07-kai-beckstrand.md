## Who is Kai Beckstrand



Reality television personality and athlete who is recognized for having competed on the first season of American Ninja Warrior Junior. He went on to win in the 11-12 year old division of the competition.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He became a fan of American Ninja Warrior at the age of 6 and trained on a homemade obstacle course in his backyard.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was diagnosed with type-1 diabetes and used his platform to raise awareness of the disease.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kai Beckstrand



His parents Brian and Holly Beckstrand founded a training facility called Grip Fitness Center.

                
                
                
## People Related With Kai Beckstrand



He competed on American Ninja Warrior Junior which was hosted by Matt Iseman.
