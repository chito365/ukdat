## Who is Katt Williams



American comedian, actor, and occasional rapper known for his role as Money Mike in Friday After Next and for his outlandish, energetic stage persona. He also did a stint on Nick Cannon&#8217;s MTV improv show Wild &#8216;N Out and appeared in the 2007 Eddie Murphy film Norbit.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He had his breakout comedy performance on the BET comedy series Comic View. He performed in his first comedy special, Katt Williams: Live: Let a Playa Play, in 2006.

                
                
                
## Random data



In 2012, he had an infamously horrible visit to Seattle, Washington, that caused him to retire, though he returned three days later.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Katt Williams



He has one biological child, Micah, from a former marriage, and seven adopted children. In 2010, he married Eboni Gray.

                
                
                
## People Related With Katt Williams



He starred in the 2008 film First Sunday with Ice Cube.
