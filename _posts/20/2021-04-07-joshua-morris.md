## Who is Joshua Morris



Web star who gained fame for posting pop culture-related videos on his YouTube account, JeebsTVVids. He grew his online presence with the help of his brother, popular YouTube rapper MattyB.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He posted his first YouTube video in November 2013.

                
                
                
## Random data



He posted several videos in collaboration with his younger brother, MattyB, including one of them competing against each other in a dance battle.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joshua Morris



His parents are Tawny Morris and Charles &#8220;Blake&#8221; Morris. His cousin posts rap videos on YouTube under the name MarsRaps. In addition to his brother Matthew, he also has siblings named Blake Jr., John and Sarah.
