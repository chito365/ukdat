## Who is Katy Bellotte



Well known for her thriving YouTube channel HelloKatyxo, she is a beauty, fashion, and lifestyle guru with more than 470,000 subscribers. She also created a blog called The Katy Project.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She started creating her videos for YouTube when she was 14 years old.

                
                
                
## Random data



Two of her most popular videos are titled &#8220;Things Guys Wish Girls Knew #1&#8221; and &#8220;Things Guys Wish Girls Knew #2.&#8221; Her popular &#8220;Things Guys Wish Girls Knew&#8221; videos became an ongoing Q&A series. She enrolled at Elon University as part of the class of 2018.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Katy Bellotte
