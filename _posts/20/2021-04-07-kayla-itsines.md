## Who is Kayla Itsines



Personal trainer for women who created the Bikini Body Training Guide and popular Sweat With Kayla app. She became a social media phenomenon with over 12 million Instagram followers as well as strong fanbases on Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She graduated from the Australian Institute of Fitness in 2008 and began working at a women&#8217;s only personal training center. She then moved onto mobile personal training before opening her own personal training studio in Adelaide.

                
                
                
## Random data



She frequently does fitness tours and has been featured in Forbes, Bloomberg, and Self, among other publications.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kayla Itsines



She is from Adelaide, Australia and has a sister named Leah. She was engaged to her business partner Tobias Pearce; they announced their separation in 2020.

                
                
                
## People Related With Kayla Itsines



 She has trained celebrities such as Paula Faris and Candace Cameron-Bure.
