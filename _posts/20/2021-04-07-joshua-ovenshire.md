## Who is Joshua Ovenshire



Gamer who became one of the six faces of the popular SmoshGames YouTube channel. He created a personal YouTube account in 2012 where he has over 430,000 subscribers. He became popular on Instagram as well with over 460,000 followers.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He uploaded videos with ClevverGames in 2010 before joining SmoshGames in 2012.

                
                
                
## Random data



He gained reality fame as a competitor on the TBS gaming competition King of the Nerds.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joshua Ovenshire



He was romantically linked to Erin Darling in 2013. In 2017, he married Katherine Bow. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Joshua Ovenshire
