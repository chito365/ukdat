## Who is Judah Nelson



Film and television actor who rose to fame after being cast as Walter Burgundy in the comedy Anchorman 2: The Legend Continues. In 2017, he guest starred on an episode of Insecure.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He made his professional acting debut on a 2011 episode of Portlandia.

                
                
                
## Random data



In 2014, he lent his voice to an episode of the animated series Over the Garden Wall.
