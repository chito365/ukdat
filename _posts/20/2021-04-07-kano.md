## Who is Kano



Award-winning grime artist known for high-charting singles like 2005&#8217;s &#8220;Typical Me&#8221; and 2007&#8217;s &#8220;This is the Girl.&#8221; He has also acted, appearing in the Channel 4 drama Top Boy as well as starring in the 2012 film Tower Block.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was previously a member of the influential grime crew N.A.S.T.Y.

                
                
                
## Random data



London mayor Ken Livingstone named him as one of London&#8217;s Heroes of 2005. He also won Best Newcomer at the 2005 MOBO awards and Best Album in 2016.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kano



His birth name is Kane Robinson. He was a soccer player growing up, playing for clubs like Chelsea and West Ham United.

                
                
                
## People Related With Kano



He collaborated with Blur and Gorillaz frontman Damon Albarn on the 2007 album London Town.
