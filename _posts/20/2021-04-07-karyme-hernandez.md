## Who is Karyme Hernández



Award-winning Mexican actress who became known for her roles in telenovelas such as Esperanza del Corazon (aka Hoping Heart), Que Bonito Amor, and Por Simper mi Amor.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She has loved to sing, dance, and act since childhood.

                
                
                
## Random data



She won Best Young Lead Actress at the 2000 Premios TVyNovelas Awards for her role in Tres Mujeres.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Karyme Hernández



She is the niece of actor Jaime Lozano and cousin of his son, former Mexican soccer player Jaime Lozano.

                
                
                
## People Related With Karyme Hernández



She appeared on the TV series Simply Maria, starring Claudia Álvarez.
