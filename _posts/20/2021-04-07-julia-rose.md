## Who is Julia Rose



Notable for her performances in the films Sweet Old World and The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, she is also recognized for her guest spots on the American television shows House M.D., The Lying Game, and CSI: NY.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Before beginning her acting career, she earned a degree in diplomatic history from the University of Pennsylvania.

                
                
                
## Random data



She made her film acting debut in the 2000 film Leading with the Right. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Julia Rose



She was born to a Turkish actress mother and a Norwegian diplomat father. She and Stephen McHugh started an on-and-off relationship in 2016.
