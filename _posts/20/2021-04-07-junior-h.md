## Who is Junior H



Latin music recording artist who rose to fame with songs like &#8220;Mente Positiva&#8221; and &#8220;Disfruto Lo Malo.&#8221; In 2020, he was profiled by the music publication Billboard.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He began writing music at the age of twelve with a friend. He taught himself to play guitar using YouTube tutorials.

                
                
                
## Random data



He worked at a sandwich shop until landing his first viral hit at the age of 17 with &#8220;No he Cambiado.&#8221; He was nominated for Radar Artist at the 2020 Spotify Awards. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Junior H



He was born in Mexico but relocated to Utah with his family at an early age. His real name is Antonio Herrera Perez. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Junior H



He and fellow rapper C-Kan were both born in Mexico.
