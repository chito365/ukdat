## Who is Katheryn Balboa



Cuban-American YouTube star whose daily life vlogs have amassed her channel 60,000 subscribers and 3.3 million overall views. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Her first YouTube video was uploaded on July 13, 2017, and was titled &#8220;My Movie 2&#8221;. 

                
                
                
## Random data



On March 14, 2019, she uploaded the video &#8220;THROWING A DART AT A MAP & BUYING WHATEVER IT LANDS ON CHALLENGE !&#8221;. It became one of her more popular videos, garnering over 60 thousand views in less than 2 months.
