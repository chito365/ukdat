## Who is Judd Nelson



His role as the rebellious John Bender in The Breakfast Club launched his career. This Brat Pack member also portrayed Alec Newbary in St. Elmo&#8217;s Fire and provided the voice of Hot Rod in Transformers.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He graduated from St. Paul&#8217;s School and attended Haverford College. After dropping out of college, he moved to New York to study acting with Stella Adler.

                
                
                
## Random data



A John Hughes film launched his career, but he also became known as Jack Richmond in Suddenly Susan.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Judd Nelson



His father served as the president of the Portland Symphony Orchestra. His sisters are Julie and Eve. He dated actress Shannen Doherty in 1993.

                
                
                
## People Related With Judd Nelson



He starred in The Breakfast Club with Emilio Estevez.
