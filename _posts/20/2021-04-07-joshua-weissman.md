## Who is Joshua Weissman



Chef and author who is known for sharing his recipes and cooking tutorials through YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok; his TikTok account has accumulated more than 4.6 million followers. He is also known for having published the Slim Palate Paleo Cookbook which was released in March of 2014. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He first started his cooking blog Slim Palate at the age of 16 wit the intent of developing healthier recipes. He gained attention after having lost 100 pounds at the age of 16. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He and his recipes have been featured on several other food blogs such as Not Without Salt, My New Roots, and Bakers Royale. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joshua Weissman



While he was born in Los Angeles, he later settled in Austin, Texas. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Joshua Weissman



He received a quote from fellow food author Michelle Tam when he released his cookbook.
