## Who is Laura Marano



Disney star who became known for her role as Ally on the show Austin and Ally after appearing in Without a Trace and Back to You. She&#8217;s also had roles in Bad Hair Day and was cast as the lead in the films A Sort of Homecoming, The Perfect Date and Saving Zoë. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She started acting in plays at the age of five, and played small roles in Ghost Whisperer and Medical Investigation. 

                
                
                
## Random data



Prior to her Disney career, she starred in the film The Jacket with Adrien Brody.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Laura Marano



She is the sister of Vanessa Marano, and both appeared in the Showtime series Dexter together. Her parents are named Ellen and Damiano. She has a dog named Velvet. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Laura Marano



She appeared in Judd Apatow&#8217;s 2007 film Superbad.
