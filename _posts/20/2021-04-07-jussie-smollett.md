## Who is Jussie Smollett



Former child actor who is known for his early &#8217;90s roles in The Might Ducks and North. In 2012, he played Magnus in The Skinny and in 2015 he began playing Jamal Lyon on the FOX series Empire. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity
