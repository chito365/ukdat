## Who is Kamri Peterson



Professional dancer chosen to be one of partners of Dancing with the Stars: Juniors for the 2018 season. She is a United States Under 10 Vice Champion as well as two-time United States Under 12 Finalist. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She trained at the Center Stage Performing Arts Studio.

                
                
                
## Random data



She was featured in Dance Spirit Magazine in March of 2018.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kamri Peterson



She has siblings named Chloe, Nixon, Nolan, and Sloan. Their parents are Hilary and Colby Peterson. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Kamri Peterson



She was featured on Dancing with the Stars: Juniors along with Addison Osta Smith.
