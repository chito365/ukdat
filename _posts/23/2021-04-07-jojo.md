## Who is JoJo



Born Joanna Noëlle Blagden Levesque, she became the youngest solo artist to release a #1 hit with &#8220;Leave (Get Out)&#8221; when she was thirteen.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She competed on the TV show, America&#8217;s Most Talented Kids, and performed on Oprah and Maury.

                
                
                
## Random data



She sang &#8220;Too Little Too Late,&#8221; which reached Platinum status in 2007. Her debut film appearance was in Aquamarine. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of JoJo



Born to parents Joel Levesque and Diana Blagden, her mother was a church soloist and theater performer. She dated soccer player Freddy Adu from May 2005 until September 2006.

                
                
                
## People Related With JoJo



She starred in the 2006 comedy RV with Robin Williams.
