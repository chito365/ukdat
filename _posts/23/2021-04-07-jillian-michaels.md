## Who is Jillian Michaels



Personal trainer who showcased her tough style as the host of The Biggest Loser. Her books include Slim for Life and Master Your Metabolism.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She worked as a bartender while supporting herself through college at California State University, Northridge.

                
                
                
## Random data



She is a famous trainer who uses a blend of kickboxing, yoga, Pilates, plyometrics, and weight training.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jillian Michaels



She got engaged to longtime partner Heidi Rhoades in 2016, but they broke off their engagement in 2019. She adopted a daughter named Lukensia from Haiti in May of 2012. That same month, she gave birth to a son named Phoenix.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jillian Michaels



Her athletic training style was showcased on the TV show The Biggest Loser, hosted by Alison Sweeney.
