## Who is Joe Flacco



Quarterback who was named MVP of Super Bowl XLVII after leading the Baltimore Ravens to a 34-31 victory over the San Francisco 49ers.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He had a dominant run at the University of Delaware, throwing for 42 touchdowns and only 1 interception in 2007. He spent his first two years of college at the University of Pittsburgh. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He became the first rookie quarterback to win two playoff games, and the first quarterback to surpass a passer rating of 100 in four consecutive postseason games. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joe Flacco



He married Dana Grady in 2011 and he has two sons named Stephen and Daniel.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joe Flacco



Ray Lewis anchored Baltimore&#8217;s defense in Super Bowl XLVII, while Flacco led the offense.
