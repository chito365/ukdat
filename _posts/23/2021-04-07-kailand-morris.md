## Who is Kailand Morris



Social media star and musician who is most well known as the son of legendary R&B singer Stevie Wonder. He has earned over 240,000 followers on Instagram, where he has promoted his music and posted pictures of his travels. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



His mother, Kai Millard Morris, was his father&#8217;s second wife. The couple were married in 2001, though they would separate in 2015. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He earned the nickname Wonderboy. He has performed as a drummer on stage with his father. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kailand Morris



He has eight siblings in total. They include Aisha, Kwame, Sophia, Keita, Mumtaz, Mandla, and Nia. He began dating Maddie Ziegler after meeting on Dancing with the Stars: Juniors in late 2018.
