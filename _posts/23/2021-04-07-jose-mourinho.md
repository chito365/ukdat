## Who is José Mourinho



Soccer manager who led Inter Milan from 2008 to 2010 and Real Madrid from 2010 to 2013 before beginning his second stint at the helm of Chelsea FC in 2013. He served as manager of Manchester United from 2016 until 2018. His successful managerial career has led to many league titles and accolades.
