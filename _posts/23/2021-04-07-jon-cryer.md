## Who is Jon Cryer



Finally broke through on television after a couple failed shows as a co-star alongside Charlie Sheen on the CBS sitcom smash hit, Two and a Half Men.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He skipped college and left to study acting in London after graduating high school.

                
                
                
## Random data

## Family & Everyday Life of Jon Cryer



In 1999, he married Sarah Trigger with whom he had one child. Following their divorce, he married Lisa Joyner in 2007, and the couple adopted a daughter in 2009.
