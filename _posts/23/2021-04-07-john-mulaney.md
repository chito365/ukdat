## Who is John Mulaney



Stand-up comedian and former writer for Saturday Night Live who is also known from VH1&#8217;s Best Week Ever. In 2012, his stand-up comedy special New In Town premiered on Comedy Central, and, in 2014, his TV series Mulaney debuted on FOX.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He studied English literature at Georgetown University.

                
                
                
## Random data



He is the co-creator of the recurring Saturday Night Live character Stefon, played by Bill Hader. In 2015, his comedy special The Comeback Kid premiered on Netflix.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Mulaney



He married Annamarie Tendler in 2014.

                
                
                
## People Related With John Mulaney



He and fellow comedian Nick Kroll became frequent collaborators. He has appeared several times on Late Night with Conan O&#8217;Brien to perform stand up comedy.
