## Who is Julie Chen



Television personality who became recognized as the host of Big Brother and The Talk. She also appeared as an anchor for The Early Show on CBS. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She began her career by interning at CBS Morning News in 1990. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She became the longest-serving host of all versions of the Big Brother franchise. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Julie Chen



She married Leslie Moonves, president and CEO of CBS Television, in December 2004. They later welcomed a son named Charlie. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Julie Chen
