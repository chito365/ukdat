## Who is John DeLuca



American actor who played the role of Butchy in the Disney Channel movie Teen Beach Movie in 2013. He also starred in the 2012 film Hemingway as the lead character, Colin Hemingway. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He graduated from Fordham University as a theatre major in 2008. 

                
                
                
## Random data
