## Who is Karla Jara



With more than 1.3 million followers, she is an Instagram phenomenon affiliated with the boutique Princess P Jewelry. She also co-runs the joint YouTube channel Kb and Karla alongside her partner KB.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She uploaded a first picture to her Instagram, a photo of Max pink sneakers, in August of 2012.

                
                
                
## Random data
