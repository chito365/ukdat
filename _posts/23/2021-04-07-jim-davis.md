## Who is Jim Davis



Cartoonist whose famously sarcastic cat, Garfield, first appeared in 1978. His other works include Gnorm Gnat and Tumbleweeds.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He grew up on a small farm in Indiana with his parents, brother, and twenty-five cats.

                
                
                
## Random data

## Family & Everyday Life of Jim Davis
