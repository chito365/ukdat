## Who is Julia Barretto



Filipina actress who has had starring roles in many television series and won the Most Stylish Rising Star at the Candy Style Awards in 2013.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She went to Miriam College for two years and then started studying at St. Paul College Pasig.

                
                
                
## Random data

## Family & Everyday Life of Julia Barretto
