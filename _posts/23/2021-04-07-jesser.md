## Who is Jesser



NBA 2K gamer who creates challenge and wager videos in addition to a Road to the Playoffs series and the occasional NBA 2K in Real Life videos. His YouTube channel has earned over 3.2 million subscribers. He later became a member of the NBA 2K-focused web group 2Hype.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He uploaded his first video on August 14, 2013.

                
                
                
## Random data



He is an avid fan of the Los Angeles Clippers. He played James Harden 1-on-1 in a video on his YouTube channel in August 2017. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jesser



He was born and raised Jesse Riedel in Los Angeles, California. He has featured his brother James, also known as Jiedel, in his videos. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jesser
