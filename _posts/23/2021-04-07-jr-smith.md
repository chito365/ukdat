## Who is JR Smith



NBA shooting guard known for his streaky three-point shooting. He was named the NBA&#8217;s Sixth Man of the Year with the New York Knicks in 2012 and in 2016, he helped lead the Cleveland Cavaliers to an NBA Championship. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He played in the McDonald&#8217;s All-American Game in 2004 after a solid career at Saint Benedict&#8217;s Preparatory School in Newark, New Jersey. He was selected 18th overall by the New Orleans Hornets in the 2004 NBA Draft. 

                
                
                
## Random data



During the 2011-12 NBA lockout, he played for the Zhejiang Golden Bulls in the Chinese Basketball Association. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of JR Smith



He played basketball overseas in China and had his mom visit often. He married Jewel Smith and he has two daughters. 

                
                
                
## People Related With JR Smith



He was teammates with Carmelo Anthony on the Knicks from 2012 to 2015.
