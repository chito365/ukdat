## Who is Jessica Shannon



Nicknamed Chubbs, she is the sister of Alana Thompson, AKA Honey Boo Boo. She appeared on the TLC reality series Here Comes Honey Boo Boo.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was born to parents David Ford and June Shannon, the latter of whom appeared on Honey Boo Boo as a series regular.

                
                
                
## Random data



She appeared on the TLC reality series Toddlers & Tiaras in 2012.
