## Who is Kamie Crawford



Model and beauty queen who was crowned Miss Teen USA 2010 and won the Miss Photogenic award the same year. She has gone on to model for JAG Models and host People Magazine&#8217;s Style Tracks as well as the web show TheRealKamie TV.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was captain of the varsity cheerleading team at Winston Churchill High School in Potomac, Maryland.

                
                
                
## Random data



She is the first person from Maryland ever to win the Miss Teen USA title. She became co-host of Catfish alongside Nev Schulman on the eighth season of the show.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Kamie Crawford



She is the oldest of five daughters born to Victor and Carla Crawford.

                
                
                
## People Related With Kamie Crawford



She shared an apartment with Miss Universe winner Ximena Navarrete and Miss USA winner Rima Fakih for a year.
