## Who is Jeroen Van Holland



YouTube vlogger who is known for his self-titled channel. He has gained widespread popularity there for his challenge completions, personal vlog entries, and friend tags.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He began his YouTube channel on November 6, 2013. He posted his first video there on July 11, 2014.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has amassed more than 300,000 subscribers to his YouTube channel since its beginning. One of his most popular videos there, &#8220;IS HET WAAR?,&#8221; has earned more than 100,000 total views.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jeroen Van Holland
