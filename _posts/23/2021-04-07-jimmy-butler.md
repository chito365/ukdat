## Who is Jimmy Butler



NBA shooting guard/small forward who had his breakout year with the Chicago Bulls during the 2014-15 season, earning the NBA&#8217;s Most Improved Player award. In 2017, he was traded to the Minnesota Timberwolves and a year later he signed with the Philadelphia 76ers. He began playing for the Miami Heat in 2019 and he helped lead the team to the 2020 NBA Finals. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was drafted 30th overall by the Bulls in 2011 after playing four seasons at Marquette University.

                
                
                
## Random data



He nearly tripled his rookie season points per game total during his sophomore 2012-2013 NBA season and continued to improve as Chicago provided him with more minutes.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jimmy Butler



His brother Jordan Leslie played football for the University of Texas, El Paso.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jimmy Butler



He joined Derrick Rose on the Chicago Bulls in 2011.
