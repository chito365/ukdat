## Who is Jordan Taylor



Widely known as the co-creator and regular contributor of the Blimey Cow YouTube comedy channel. He, alongside his brother Josh, have gained popularity for their mockumentary-style sketches and original satire.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He co-created his YouTube channel on June 27, 2006. He posted the first video there the same day.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has helped the Blimey Cow channel amass more than 500,000 subscribers. On June 26, 2015, he released his debut album, Long Drive, to iTunes.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jordan Taylor



He has a sister named Amy and his sister-in-law is Kelli Taylor. He married longtime girlfriend Sara in May 2017.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jordan Taylor



He and his brother made a video for religious author and personality Thomas Woods called &#8220;How to Answer a Government Propagandist.&#8221;
