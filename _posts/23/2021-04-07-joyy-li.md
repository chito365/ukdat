## Who is Joyy.li



TikTok star and influencer who is best recognized for posting colorful lip synch videos as well as short modeling clips for her over 1.4 million followers. She is also known for her dance videos and clips that showcase her fashion sense which have contributed to her over 41 million total likes. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She first started sharing videos through her TikTok account in January of 2019. She went viral in May when a video of her taking a panorama picture earned over 150,000 views and several ForYou page features. 

                
                
                
## Random data
