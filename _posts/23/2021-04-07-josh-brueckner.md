## Who is Josh Brueckner



Pro Middleweight MMA fighter whose fighter nickname is G.I. Josh Brueckner. He gained additional fame as one half of the popular couple vlog Jatie Vlogs on YouTube. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He&#8217;s originally from Michigan and has been associated with the Michigan Top Team gym in Dearborn, Michigan. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He&#8217;s is the 9th ranked of 26 active Michigan Pro Middleweights, and he&#8217;s also worked as a title boxing instructor and personal trainer.
