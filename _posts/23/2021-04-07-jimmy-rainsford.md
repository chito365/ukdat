## Who is Jimmy Rainsford



Drummer and musician who is best recognized for his work with the two piece pop rock band Picture This. He is also known for uploading videos of him playing drums along with tutorial content to YouTube for his over 60,000 subscribers. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



Beginning as childhood friends, he and Ryan Hennessy began playing music together early on and formed the band Picture This in October of 2015. After their song &#8220;Take My Hand&#8221; went viral online, their first show in Dublin sold out in under 30 minutes. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He was featured in an article in The Irish Times in April of 2016 where he was interviewed about his accomplishment of selling out the The Academy venue for his debut live show. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jimmy Rainsford



He was born and raised in Athy, Ireland.
