## Who is Jenna Joseph



Best known as the wife of Twenty One Pilots lead vocalist Tyler Joseph.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was the sister-in-law of a guy Tyler went to high school with. The couple became engaged on July 7, 2014.
