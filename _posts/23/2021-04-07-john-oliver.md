## Who is John Oliver



British comedian and actor best known for his appearances as a correspondent on The Daily Show with Jon Stewart. He had a recurring role on the NBC sitcom Community, and in 2014 he began hosting the show Last Week Tonight on HBO.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



After graduating from university, he debuted his comedy at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival and later moved to New York to continue pursuing stand-up.

                
                
                
## Random data



He spent eight weeks in 2013 hosting The Daily Show when Jon Stewart was away.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Oliver



He met his wife Kate Norley at the 2008 Republican National Convention. The two were married in 2011 and had a son named Hudson in 2015.

                
                
                
## People Related With John Oliver



He and Stephen Colbert became two of The Daily Show&#8217;s most successful former correspondents.
