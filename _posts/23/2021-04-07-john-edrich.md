## Who is John Edrich



Fearless batsman considered one of the finest of his generation, represented England in Tests from 1963 to 1976, Member of the British Royal Empire.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was a military man who played cricket for the Combined Services in 1956 and 1957.
