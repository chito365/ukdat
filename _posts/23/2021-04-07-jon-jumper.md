## Who is Jon Jumper



Singer and producer who became known as the mastermind behind the crunkcore project Unicorns Killed My Girlfriend. He later embarked on a solo career under the stage name Lil Zim and achieved a new level of fame when his music video for &#8220;Problem&#8221; went viral in August 2016.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was inspired by artists like Brokencyde and The Millionaires.

                
                
                
## Random data



He released the collaborative EP Rawr XD with Scarlet Skyie in 2016.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jon Jumper



He dated Roxie Love, whom he met when they worked together in Unicorns Killed My Girlfriend. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jon Jumper
