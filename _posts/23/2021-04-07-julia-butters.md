## Who is Julia Butters



Child actress with notable credits on television series such as Transparent and American Housewife. She was cast in the film 13 Hours: The Secret Soldiers of Benghazi as Beverly Silva. She appeared as Trudi in Quentin Tarantino&#8217;s 2019 film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She made her television debut for a speaking part in a 2014 episode of Criminal Minds. She had previously been seen in print and TV advertisements. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She had a recurring role in a couple episodes of Best Friends Whenever. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Julia Butters



She is the daughter of Lorelei and Darrin Butters.

                
                
                
## People Related With Julia Butters
