## Who is Jesse Polock



Lifeguard famous for his appearances on the Australian Channel Ten reality series Bondi Rescue. His Instagram account has attracted more than 90,000 followers.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was a member of the infamous Bra Boy gang and had several run-ins with the law.

                
                
                
## Random data



For the documentary The Ride East Coast, he made a 1,800-mile journey from Sydney to Cairns via jetski, stopping along the way at youth mental health centers to raise awareness about suicide prevention.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jesse Polock



He grew up in the beachside suburb of Sydney known as Maroubra. He began a relationship with model Tulle Zimmerman and they have a son together. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jesse Polock



He and fellow lifeguard Harrison Reid are two of the stars of Bondi Rescue.
