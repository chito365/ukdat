## Who is John Tyler



Tenth U.S. president and the first to succeed a president because of the officeholder&#8217;s death. He led the way for the annexation of Texas in 1845.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was raised with his two brothers and five sisters on Greenway Plantation in Virginia. He started practicing law when he was 19 years old.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was an adamant advocate of states&#8217; rights and supported the Confederate government during the Civil War, serving in the Confederate House of Representatives until his death.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Tyler



He married twice, fathering eight children with his first wife and seven more with his second.

                
                
                
## People Related With John Tyler



He succeeded William Henry Harrison, who served as president for only 31 days before dying of complications caused by pneumonia.
