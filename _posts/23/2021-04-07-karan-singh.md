## Who is Karan Singh



This Indian National Congress Party leader held office as Governor of India&#8217;s Jammu and Kashmir state and went on to serve in the Rajya Sabha house of the Indian Parliament.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



After graduating from Sri Pratap College Srinagar, he earned graduate political science degrees from Delhi University.

                
                
                
## Random data



He was the 2005 recipient of the prestigious Padma Vibhushan honor.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Karan Singh



He was born in Cannes, France, to Jammu and Kashmir ruler Hari Singh.

                
                
                
## People Related With Karan Singh



He and Omar Abdullah both represented Jammu and Kashmir in the Indian Parliament; Abdullah served in the lower house and Singh in the upper.
