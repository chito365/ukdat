## Who is Joe Jitsukawa



Co-founder, writer, director, and actor for the enormously popular Just Kidding Films team, a YouTube channel that takes on social, contemporary, and cultural issues with a comedic edge. He would make a personal YouTube channel called Joe Jitsukawa featuring life vlogs, cooking videos and rants that earned 490,000 subscribers. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He studied psychology at Cal State Los Angeles. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He has a personal channel on YouTube as well where he posts vlogs, music, philosophies, random acts of dancing and more.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joe Jitsukawa



He was born in California, but has lived all over the world in Korea, Australia, Texas, Michigan, Japan, China, Taiwan and Thailand. He dated his fellow film partner Jessica Caldwell.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joe Jitsukawa



He and his partner Bart Kwan founded JKF while attending the UCLA. Dancer Anthony Lee has starred in skits on Just Kidding Films.
