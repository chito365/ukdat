## Who is Jordan Henderson



Midfielder who joined Liverpool in 2011 and was named England&#8217;s U-21 Player of the Year in 2012. He became a member of the English national team in 2010.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He made his professional debut with Sunderland in 2008 and also spent time on loan to Coventry City.

                
                
                
## Random data



He played for England&#8217;s U-21 team in the 2011 European Championships.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jordan Henderson



He was born in Sunderland, England. He married Rebecca Burnett. He and Rebecca have two daughters named Elexa and Alba and in 2020, they welcomed a son. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jordan Henderson



He was teammates with Steven Gerrard on Liverpool and took over the latter&#8217;s captaincy in 2015.
