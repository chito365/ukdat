## Who is Judith Jaso



Spanish beauty guru known for her YouTube channel ItsJudith. Her collection of challenges, vlogs, and hauls have earned her more than 850,000 subscribers. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



In November 2011, she opened her Twitter account with the opening tweet &#8220;Todos ven lo que aparentas, pero pocos ven lo que realmente eres.&#8221;

                
                
                
## Random data



One of her most popular YouTube videos is &#8220;¡PROBANDO DULCES AMERICANOS CON MI HERMANO!,&#8221; which earned more than 1 million views in less than a year.
