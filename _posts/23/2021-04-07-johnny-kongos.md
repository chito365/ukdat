## Who is Johnny Kongos



One of four members of the all-sibling alternative rock band KONGOS who plays the accordion and keyboards for the group, in addition to providing vocals. Their debut album was self-titled, and their follow ups are titled Lunatic and Egomaniac.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He attended Arizona State University where he studied jazz. He studied classical piano previously.

                
                
                
## Random data



The group&#8217;s song &#8220;Come with Me Now&#8221; was featured in the trailers of video games Borderlands: The Pre-Sequel and Payday 2, and in the trailer for the film Holy Motors.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Johnny Kongos



He and the family moved to Phoenix, Arizona from London after being born in South Africa. His father is John Kongos.

                
                
                
## People Related With Johnny Kongos



He performs alongside siblings Dylan, Daniel and Jesse Kongos.
