## Who is Jonathan Scott



HGTV personality who is known as the face of Property Brothers and Buying and Selling. He hosts both shows alongside his brother Drew Scott.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He began performing in local theater productions at age seven.

                
                
                
## Random data



He has been a presenter on four consecutive Annual Leo Awards and was the host for the 2009 ceremony.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jonathan Scott



Apart from Drew, he has another brother named JD. He dated Jacinta Kuznetsov from 2015 until 2018. He began dating Zooey Deschanel in 2019. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jonathan Scott



He guest-starred on three episode of The Marilyn Denis Show in 2011.
