## Who is Jessica Jackson



Singer, social media influencer, and video content creator who earned her most substantial following on the app TikTok, where she has more than 850,000 fans on her imjessjackson account.

She sang a rendition of &#8220;We Shall Overcome&#8221; during her elementary school recital of of the Martin Luther King Speech. She went to school to earn a certification in Medical Office Assisting.
