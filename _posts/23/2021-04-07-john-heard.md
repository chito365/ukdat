## Who is John Heard



Television and film actor who is best known for his role as father Peter McCallister in the Home Alone films. His other films include Chilly Scenes of Winter, Sharknado, Cutter&#8217;s Way, and The Pelican Brief. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He graduated from Clark University.  

                
                
                
## Random data



He made recurring television appearances on The Client, The Sopranos, Jack & Bobby and CSI: Miami.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of John Heard



He divorced his third wife, Lana Pritchard, in 2010. He had children named Max, John and Annika. 

                
                
                
## People Related With John Heard



He played Governor Frank Tancredi in the 2005 season of Prison Break with Wentworth Miller.
