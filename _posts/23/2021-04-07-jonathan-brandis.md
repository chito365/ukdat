## Who is Jonathan Brandis



Child actor whose career soared after he appeared in the 1990 film The Never Ending Story II, which also earned him a Saturn Award nomination for Best Performance by a Younger Actor. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He worked as a child model and commercial actor growing up. 

                
                
                
## Random data



He made his first film appearance as a party guest in the 1987 film Fatal Attraction. He won the 1994 Young Artist Award for &#8220;Best Youth Actor Leading Role in a Television Series&#8221; for his role in seaQuest DSV.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jonathan Brandis



He dated actress Tatyana Ali from 1995 to 1998. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jonathan Brandis



He was a guest star on Full House with Bob Saget.
