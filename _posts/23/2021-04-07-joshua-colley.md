## Who is Joshua Colley



Actor, singer and voice over artist who has starred on Broadway in the revival of Les Misérables and in Disney&#8217;s Newsies. He was also seen in Elf The Musical at Madison Square Garden.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



His professional acting career began at the age of nine.

                
                
                
## Random data



He provided the voice of Pig Robinson on the Nick Jr. television show Peter Rabbit, and the voice of The Shadow King in Nick Jr.&#8217;s Team Umizoomi.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joshua Colley



He was born and raised in Florida. He has a twin brother named Cameron.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joshua Colley
