## Who is Joe Montana



Legendary San Francisco 49ers quarterback who led the team to four Super Bowl victories after winning a national championship at Notre Dame. He was named Super Bowl MVP three times, selected for the Pro Bowl eight times, and chosen for the 1980s All-Decade Team. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He was invited to Parade magazine&#8217;s All-American team after two sterling years as the starting quarterback for the Ringgold High School Rams.

                
                
                
## Random data
