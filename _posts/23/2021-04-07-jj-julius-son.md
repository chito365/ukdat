## Who is JJ Julius Son



Lead vocalist and a guitarist for the Icelandic rock band Kaleo that released a self-titled debut in 2013, and a follow-up called A/B in 2016.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He and the band first made a name for themselves at the 2012 Iceland Airwaves Music Festival. Their first hit song was called &#8220;Vor í Vaglaskógi.&#8221;

                
                
                
## Random data
