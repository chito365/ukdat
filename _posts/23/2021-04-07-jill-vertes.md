## Who is Jill Vertes



Mother of dancer Kendall Vertes who stars on the hit reality show Dance Moms. Her first appearance came in the second season of the show. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was a competitive cheerleader in high school and then cheered for the University of Pittsburgh. She graduated from Pitt with a Bachelor of Arts. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She took first place at a competition called Dancing with the Celebrities of Pittsburgh in May 2013. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jill Vertes



She married Erno Valer Vertes. In addition to Kendall, she has two other daughters named Ryleigh and Charlotte. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jill Vertes



She became friends with fellow Dance Mom Melissa Ziegler.
