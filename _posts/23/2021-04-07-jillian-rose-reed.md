## Who is Jillian Rose Reed



American actress who was nominated for a Young Artist Award for her lead role in the MTV teen series Awkward. She later lent her voice to the Disney series Elena of Avalor.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She had a recurring role as the character Simone on the Showtime series Weeds. 

                
                
                
## Random data



She was in the cast of My Super Psycho Sweet 16: Part 3, a 2012 TV movie.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jillian Rose Reed



Her younger brother, Robbie Tucker, appeared on the popular CBS soap opera The Young and the Restless. She also has an older brother named Matt.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jillian Rose Reed



She appeared alongside actress Mary Louise Parker in seven episodes of the popular television series Weeds.
