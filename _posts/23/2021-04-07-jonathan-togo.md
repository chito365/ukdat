## Who is Jonathan Togo



Played the role of Ryan Wolfe on the television series CSI: Miami and also played a role in the film Mystic River.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He studied theater at Vasaar College, where he also performed in a band that became The Bravery.

                
                
                
## Random data



In addition to his on-screen work, he appeared in many stage productions, winning the Margaret Thatcher Kazan Award for his role in Our Country&#8217;s Good.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jonathan Togo



He was born in Rockland, Massachusetts, and his father, Michael Togo, was a graphic designer who shortened his family name from Tonkaviev. In 2013, he married Diora Baird, and together they have a child.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jonathan Togo
