## Who is Jourdan Dunn



Model who has worked with Prada, H&M and Calvin Klein, and appeared internationally in the pages of Vogue.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was discovered at age 16 at a local Primark by a scout from Storm Models, which also employed Kate Moss.

                
                
                
## Random data



She has continually spoken out on the difficulties faced by black models, and in 2013 she revealed one racist makeup artist who refused to touch her skin.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jourdan Dunn



Her single mother raised her and her two brothers. She has a son named Riley.

                
                
                
## People Related With Jourdan Dunn



She and fellow British fashion model Naomi Campbell walked during the 2012 Summer Olympics closing ceremony.
