## Who is Jess Folley



Pop music performer who rose to fame after competing on, and winning, the 2017 season of The Voice Kids UK. She stunned the audience and judges alike with a powerful rendition of Beyoncé&#8217;s &#8220;Love On Top&#8221; and went on to form the group Real Like You through winning The X Factor: The Band.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



She was cast as Elphaba in a school production of the musical Wicked. 

                
                
                
## Random data



Upon winning The Voice Kids, she received £30,000 to go towards furthering her music education. 

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Jess Folley



She is from Essex, England. 

                
                
                
## People Related With Jess Folley



During her time on The Voice Kids UK, she was mentored by celebrity coach Pixie Lott.
