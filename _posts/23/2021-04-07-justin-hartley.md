## Who is Justin Hartley



Actor who starred on the NBC drama series This Is Us. He&#8217;s also known for playing the role of Green Arrow on the television series Smallville and had a role on the primetime soap opera Revenge. 

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He made his acting debut on NBC&#8217;s soap opera Passions in 2002.

                
                
                
## Random data



He had the starring role in a never-aired CW television pilot of Aquaman.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Justin Hartley



He married Passions co-star Lindsay Korman in May 2004, and the couple divorced after 8 years of marriage. The couple had a daughter named Bella in 2004. He began dating actress Chrishell Stause in 2014. They married in October 2017, then divorced two years later.

                
                
                
## People Related With Justin Hartley
