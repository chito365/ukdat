## Who is Joe Bereta



One half of the popular YouTube sketch comedy group Barats and Bereta. Their channel has accumulated more than 380,000 subscribers.

                
                
                
## Prior to Popularity



He and Luke Barats first joined YouTube on December 28, 2005

                
                
                
## Random data



His channel features the video series Great Pick-Up Lines and Skitlets. He was a host for SourceFed from 2012 to 2014.

                
                
                
## Family & Everyday Life of Joe Bereta



He was born in Columbia Falls, Montana. He married Heather Bereta, and has two children, a daughter named Hayden and a son named Jackson.

                
                
                
## People Related With Joe Bereta



After being challenged by fellow YouTubers Rhett and Link, he posted a series of popular YouTube challenges all in one video in May 2013.
